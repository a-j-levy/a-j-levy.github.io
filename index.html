Start simple. Pick a style. Get a main.

Super Smash Bros. is a fast party-fighting game where every character feels different. Some are easy and balanced, while others are fast and technical or slow but powerful.

This page helps you choose a character based on the kind of gameplay you enjoy, then gives you a few beginner tips so you can actually improve instead of randomly switching every match.

If you’re new, don’t worry about “the best character.” A character you understand will usually beat a “top-tier” you don’t know how to play.

How people usually choose a main

  • Balanced

    Good all-around tools. Great for learning the basics without feeling stuck.

    See balanced fighters

  • Fast / Aggressive

    Quick movement and pressure. You trade simplicity for speed and combos.

    See fast fighters

  • Heavy / Powerful

    Big hits and survivability. Slower, but you can win with a few good reads.

    See heavy fighters

Beginner tips (toggle)

  1. Pick one character and stick with them for a week.
  2. Learn your main’s recovery options so you don’t lose stocks for free.
  3. Focus on spacing and staying calm instead of mashing.
